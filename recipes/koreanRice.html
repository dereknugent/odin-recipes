Korean Rice

  1. Cucumbers: This bibimap recipe starts with cucumbers, cut into matchsticks, mixed with gochujang (optional, but it adds subtle heat).
  2. Vegetables: You’ll also need spinach, cut into thin strips, and carrots, cut into matchsticks.
  3. Soy sauce: Soy sauce lends umami-rich flavor.
  4. Olive oil: Cook the carrots and garlic in olive oil.
  5. Seasonings: Take the flavor up a notch with fresh garlic and dried red pepper flakes.
  6. Steak: This recipe calls for thinly-sliced beef — top round steak is rich and beefy, which makes it perfect for bibimbap.
  7. Eggs: You’ll need one whole egg per serving.
  8. Rice: Ladle each serving over bibimbap over a cup of cooked white rice.
  9. Sesame oil: Toasting the sesame oil brings out a depth of flavor.
  10. Sesame seeds: A teaspoon of sesame seeds are the perfect finishing touch.
